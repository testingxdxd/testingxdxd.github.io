Our Mission

Supporting Those Who Served

Honor Haven is dedicated to assisting veterans and their spouses in meeting the strict guidelines for qualification for the VA's Aid and Attendance pension, as well as Medicaid. Our experienced representatives are here to work with you and your other professionals, ensuring you receive the benefits you deserve.

The Aid and Attendance pension is a vital resource, yet it remains underutilized. We aim to change that by providing the necessary support and guidance to veterans and their families, helping them navigate the complexities of accessing these benefits.

At Honor Haven, we understand that transitioning into assisted living or seeking in-home care can be daunting. That's why we're here to make this transition smoother and more positive for you and your loved ones.
