Meet Our Team

Kenda Pardy, CLU
Financial Advisor

Kenda Pardy is the President of Honor Haven, an Illinois-based company dedicated to helping veterans and non-veterans achieve their financial objectives. Kenda is a Life and qualifying member of the Million Dollar Round Table, the highest recognized level of accomplishment for Financial Professionals. Through Honor Haven, Kenda assists veterans and their families in qualifying for the Veterans Aid & Attendance benefit and collaborates with elder law attorneys and accountants for estate planning needs.

Kenda is also the founder of Funding Solutions, dedicated to helping non-veterans find economical methods of paying for long-term care expenses. With her expertise as a Financial Advisor, Chartered Life Underwriter, and Certified Mutual Fund Consultant, Kenda ensures exceptional professional knowledge, ethical conduct, and outstanding client service.
